About

We believe in transparency, cooperation globally, and the free exchange of talent and ideas. A forum for the sharing of these technical and humanitarian discussions is given for meetings, conferences and other events.

Conferences are a part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. We are committed to developing strategies that support and expand the conference activities throughout the world.

Established 2012

Driving Technological Advancement Globally.

GlobalCon began as a small roundtable discussion in Singapore and has since evolved into the world's most influential technology conference organization.

"Our mission is to foster innovation by connecting the world's most brilliant minds."
